Pettigrew Animal Shelter

because we care

Located in sunny Portland

Here at Pettigrew Animal Shelter we strive to serve the community and our loving furry friends (we do not help snakes so please take them elsewhere). We have been in business for over 40 years now, ever since Dr. Harmon came back from Nam really. We have helped with everything from puppy sniffles to full blown elephant open heart surgery. We definitely charge more than our competitors but just thing of how much better care we take of YOU! We cater much more to our human customers than our four-legged ones and that is because we know who pays them checks.
